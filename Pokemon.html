Pokemon

Pokémon are mysterious creatures filled with many secrets.
Some Pokémon live alongside humans and some live in the
wild in grassy fields, caves, or the sea, but much about their
ecology that remains unknown. One of their main features is
that they can be caught using a Poké Ball, which allows them
to be carried around.
